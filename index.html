ABOUT US

Key features of our think tank

Mission

Our mission is to improve education quality through technology to promote sustainable development in Nigeria.

Passion

As a think tank, we are passionate about education, women empowerment, job creation and agriculture.

Wicked Problem

On the basis of UNICEF, the literacy rate of women in Nigeria in 2020 is 63%. How can technology enhance education rate to 75% by 2035 to promote sustainable development of the country.

Creativity

Its the ability to think outside the box. We make decisions, create something new and generate a lot of ideas.

Why Education?

Technology today plays an important role in Nigerian society and the world at large. It affects the way people communicate, learn and think. It has made learning from home straightforward and has many useful applications in education too.

One of the most significant tools to empower girls within their family and community is education. Education is recognized as a fundamental human right. Gender inequality in education however remains a huge concern. Girls account for 60% of Nigeria's 10 million out-of-school children, facing barriers such as child marriage, poverty and discriminatory social norms.

As a think tank, we believe that achieving progress requires action at multiple levels — sub-national, national and global — to achieve lasting impact for girls’ education.
